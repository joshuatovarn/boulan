Data Privacy and Cookies Policy for Boûlan www.boûlan.com and shop.boûlan.com (our “site”) uses ‘cookies’ to help you personalise your online experience. A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. A cookie can be used to identify a user’s computer or internet enabled device to the website, or to other third party websites. This allows websites to improve the way they work, such as remembering preferences expressed by a user or tracking a user’s use of a website for statistical analysis.Cookies cannot be used to run programmes on your computer. The list below names the cookies we collect and what information they store. You may refuse to accept cookies by activating the settings on your browser which allows you to refuse the setting of cookies. However, if you select this setting, you may be unable to access certain parts of our site.
